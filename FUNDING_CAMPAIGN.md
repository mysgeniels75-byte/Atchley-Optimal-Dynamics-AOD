## Risk Mitigation

### Scientific Risks

**Risk**: Predictions fail empirical tests
- **Mitigation**: Refine theory based on data, publish negative results
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: High

**Risk**: Convergence issues in large-scale systems
- **Mitigation**: Develop better approximations, hybrid methods
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: Medium

**Risk**: Scooped by competing research
- **Mitigation**: Rapid publication, establish priority via preprints
- **Probability**: Low (novel theory)
- **Impact**: Medium

### Funding Risks

**Risk**: Grant applications rejected
- **Mitigation**: Apply to 10+ sources, diversify funding
- **Probability**: High (70% rejection typical)
- **Impact**: Medium

**Risk**: Crowdfunding fails to meet goals
- **Mitigation**: All-or-nothing campaigns, strong marketing
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: Low (not sole funding source)

**Risk**: Industry partnerships fall through
- **Mitigation**: Multiple simultaneous negotiations
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: Low

### Technical Risks

**Risk**: Hardware prototypes don't work
- **Mitigation**: Thorough simulation first, expert consultants
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: Medium

**Risk**: Scaling limitations discovered
- **Mitigation**: Focus on niche applications, optimize algorithms
- **Probability**: Low
- **Impact**: High

### Reputation Risks

**Risk**: Perceived as pseudoscience
- **Mitigation**: Rigorous peer review, falsifiable predictions
- **Probability**: Low (Phase 2 mitigated this)
- **Impact**: High

**Risk**: Ethical concerns about applications
- **Mitigation**: Clear ethical guidelines, refuse military contracts
- **Probability**: Low
- **Impact**: Medium
