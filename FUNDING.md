### Specific Needs

#### Tier 1: Computational Resources ($500-2,000/month)
- **GPU Clusters**: Train large-scale evolutionary algorithms (1000+ agents)
- **Cloud Computing**: AWS/GCP for distributed simulations
- **Energy Measurement**: Specialized hardware to measure actual Joules consumed
- **Database Storage**: Store simulation results, evolutionary histories

#### Tier 2: Hardware Prototyping ($5,000-20,000)
- **Memristor Arrays**: Purchase/fabricate 1k√ó1k crossbar arrays for analog computation
- **FPGA Development**: Implement AOD algorithms on reconfigurable hardware
- **Neuromorphic Chips**: Test on Intel Loihi, BrainChip Akida, IBM TrueNorth
- **Measurement Equipment**: Oscilloscopes, power analyzers for energy validation

#### Tier 3: Experimental Validation ($10,000-50,000)
- **Neuroscience Collaboration**: Partner with labs for EEG/MEG/fMRI studies
- **Behavioral Experiments**: Test predictions about decision-making, learning
- **Animal Studies**: Validate power-law structures in neural recordings (if ethical approval obtained)
- **Human Subjects**: Speed-accuracy trade-offs, cognitive load experiments

#### Tier 4: Publication & Dissemination ($2,000-10,000)
- **Open Access Fees**: $3,000-5,000 per paper in high-impact journals
- **Conference Travel**: Present at NeurIPS, ICML, Cosyne, SfN
- **Preprint Servers**: bioRxiv, arXiv hosting and DOI registration
- **Educational Materials**: Videos, tutorials, interactive demos
