**Key Principle**: Instead of competing, partner agents share information and resources to achieve higher collective performance (aligned with AOD Theory's cooperative optimization).

### Skills Network Architecture

**Specialized Agent Classes**:

1. **Production Planners** - High-level scheduling and resource allocation
2. **Quality Controllers** - Real-time defect detection and correction
3. **Energy Optimizers** - Minimize energy consumption (AOD-native)
4. **Supply Chain Coordinators** - Logistics and inventory optimization
5. **Maintenance Predictors** - Predictive maintenance scheduling
6. **Human-Robot Collaborators** - Safe human-machine coordination

**Symbiotic Interactions**:
- Energy Optimizer ↔ Production Planner: Schedule energy-intensive operations during low-cost periods
- Quality Controller ↔ Maintenance Predictor: Detect equipment degradation before defects occur
- Supply Chain ↔ Production Planner: Just-in-time coordination
- All agents ↔ AOD Core: Continuous multi-objective optimization
